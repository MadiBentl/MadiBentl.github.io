Colour Pomodoro

Pomodoro is a popular productivity tool that uses a timer to divide your time between cycles of work and breaks.

This pomodoro allows users to input their desired break and work times. True to its name, the background changes colour in between work periods and breaks.

Snake

In this classic arcade game, the snake, directed by arrow keys, moves around eating white squares to increase his length. When the snake goes offscreen or hits its own tail, the game ends.

This version is built in HTML, Javascript, jQuery and CSS. It displays a user's score for that round, and high score for the session.

Weather Wear

Never know what to wear for your jog?

Weather Wear offers you the perfect recommendation based on the weather. The web app takes the user's GIS location and returns the temperature, wind speed and conditions based on the Open Weather API.
